The Ultimate Singapore Travel Guide!

Singapore is a small city-state located in South-East Asia which borders Malaysia. The country is officially just 56 years old (!!!) which is likely younger than many of you reading this blog. The country first became an area of international interest after it established itself as the world's largest publically owned shipping port. This allowed for a constant influx of new people from different backgrounds to enter the country and leave their mark on its culture. This is partially why the population is so diverse and unique in its makeup. The Singaporean government also enacted the 'Speak Good English' movement in 2000 to promote the adoption of English as the country's main language in order to increase tourism. The movement was a huge social and cultural success and likely played a large role in bringing you to this blog.

Singapore is also known as the 'Garden city' due to how green and natural it is. Creations like the cloud dome have helped foster and mantain this image that attracts millions of visitors every year.

Halimah Yacoub is Singapore's current president and the first female president in the country's history. She has helped create a safe and prosperous environment in Singapore which has allowed the local population to flourish and tourism to increase year-in-year-out.
